# Content Pages Specification

## Overview

- Target file: `src/components/mvf/Pages.tsx`
- Screenshots: all page desktop/mobile captures under `docs/design-references/mooneevalleyfoundation/`
- Interaction model: mostly static sections, links, visual-only forms.

## Templates

- `PageBanner`: cyan title band.
- `InfoSections`: structured content sections.
- `ApplyPage`: preserved application `.doc` links.
- `ContactPage`: visual-only form and image split.
- `SupportPage`: donation placeholder.
- `PastWinnersPage`: year-grouped data.
- `NewsPage`: post-card grid.

## Responsive Behavior

- Desktop: centered constrained content and split media/content layouts.
- Mobile: stacked content and drawer navigation.
